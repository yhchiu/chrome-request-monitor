Usage Instructions

  • After setting up rules, matching URLs will automatically appear in overlay boxes on pages when you browse websites
  • You can click the extension icon to open the popup window to view all found URLs
  • Regular expression matching is case-sensitive, other match types are case-insensitive
  • It is recommended to start with simple "contains" matching, and then gradually use more complex rules
